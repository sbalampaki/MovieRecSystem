Meanings of the Ratings:

  • Approved: Approved for all audiences
  • G: All ages admitted. General audiences
  • NC-17: Adults only. No one under 17 admitted.
  • Not Rated: No rating available
  • PG: Parental guidance suggested. Some material may not be suitable for children.
  • PG-13: Parents strongly cautioned. Some material may be inappropriate for children under 13.
  • R: Restricted. Under 17 requires accompanying parent or adult guardian.
  • TV-MA: Television program is intended for mature audiences only and may be unsuitable for children under 17.
  • TV-PG: Parental guidance suggested. Some material may not be suitable for children under 14.
  • Unrated: No rating available
  • X: No one 17 and under admitted
